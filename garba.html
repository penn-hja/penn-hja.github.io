Garba

HJA hosts the largest Garba in the greater Philadelphia area. Garba is a dance tradition practiced during the Hindu festival of Navaratri. It’s not exclusive to just Garba dancing — also featuring a mix of Raas, Bhangra, and Bollywood styles as well.
